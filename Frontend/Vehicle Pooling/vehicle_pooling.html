The vehicle pooling functionality in CamBuzz offers a streamlined and efficient solution for users to share rides, reducing individual carbon footprints and promoting a sense of community. Users can create ride listings, specifying details such as departure and arrival locations, date, time, vehicle type, available seats, and pricing. Other users can then search for rides based on their travel preferences and join available listings. The system ensures a secure and personalized experience by integrating user profiles, allowing participants to view essential details about the ride owner, including their full name, branch, and contact information. Additionally, automated notifications and email alerts enhance communication, providing updates about booking changes and cancellations. Through these features, CamBuzz's vehicle pooling fosters a collaborative and eco-friendly travel ecosystem while maintaining a user-friendly and organized interface for seamless ride sharing.
